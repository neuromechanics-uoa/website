+ it's _vs_ its
+ capitalization:
    * titles
    * Parkinson's disease
+ numbers / units and spacing
+ commas
    * clauses
    * lists (the Oxford comma)
+ comprise
+ less _vs_ fewer; much _vs_ many; amount _vs_ number
+ however _vs_ but
